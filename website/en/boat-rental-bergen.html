Self-guided boat rental

Freedom on the water

Sometimes you want to set your own pace. Our boat rental lets you do exactly that — take a motorboat out onto the Byfjord and chart your own course along the coastline. Anchor in a quiet cove for lunch, explore the islands west of Bergen, or simply drift and enjoy the views at leisure.

No previous boating experience is required. Before departure, we give every group a thorough safety briefing and a practical introduction to the boat. Our friendly team is always reachable by phone if you have any questions out on the water. The boat accommodates up to 8 people, making it perfect for families, friend groups and small team outings.

Pricing is tailored to your group's needs — duration, season and any additional requests. Contact us for a personalised quote and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Who is it for

Perfect for all occasions

Families and friends

Room for up to 8 people means everyone can come along. Explore hidden beaches, go swimming in a cove or simply enjoy a picnic afloat with the people you love most.

Corporate outings

A self-guided boat trip is a relaxed, memorable way to spend time with colleagues outside the office. We can combine it with a sauna session for a full day on the fjord.

Couples and anniversaries

Rent the boat for a private afternoon on the water. Bring your own drinks and food, find a secluded spot, and let the fjord set the scene for a truly special occasion.

Flexible duration

From a quick two-hour trip to a full day out on the fjord — we tailor the rental to your schedule. Longer bookings mean a better per-hour rate.

Safe and well-maintained

Every boat undergoes regular technical inspection. All necessary safety equipment — life jackets, flares and first aid — is on board before you leave the dock.

Central location

Depart from Bryggen in the heart of Bergen. You're straight out on the open Byfjord within minutes, with the islands and coastline of western Norway at your fingertips.

What's included

Ready to go

Everything you need for a safe and enjoyable day on the water is included in the rental.

  • Fully fuelled motorboat
  • Life jackets for all passengers
  • Safety briefing and boat introduction
  • Nautical chart of the local area
  • Phone support while on the water
  • Free rebooking due to bad weather
