Floating sauna

Where heat meets the cold Norwegian sea

The Scandinavian sauna tradition is centuries old — and nowhere does it feel more alive than on a floating sauna, right on the water, surrounded by Bergen's dramatic fjord landscape. Our wood-fired badstue heats to 80–90°C and sits just a step away from the cold fjord, making the hot-cold contrast as intense and invigorating as it gets.

Whether you come for a private session with friends, a guided sauna cruise, or just a spontaneous drop-in after a long day of sightseeing, the experience is the same: deep warmth, pure cold, and a profound sense of calm that only the sea can bring. Our floating sauna is designed for year-round use — in fact, winter is many guests' favourite season, when the steam rises dramatically into the cold air.

Why the floating sauna

More than just heat

Open all year

The floating sauna is available twelve months a year. Winter is particularly spectacular — the dramatic contrast between 90°C heat and near-zero fjord water is something you experience with your entire body.

Proven wellness benefits

Regular sauna use improves circulation, reduces muscle tension, supports recovery, and promotes deeper sleep. The contrast bathing (hot-cold-hot) is especially effective for both physical recovery and mental clarity.

Unique location

Our sauna floats directly on the Byfjord with panoramic views of the Bergen skyline, the Seven Mountains and the open water. No hotel spa can offer this perspective.
