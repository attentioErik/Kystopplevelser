RIB Tour

Bergen from the sea

No other experience gives you Bergen quite like a RIB tour on the Byfjord. Speed past Bryggen's UNESCO-listed buildings, navigate hidden coves, and feel the fresh sea air as your certified guide shares local knowledge and history along the way.

Two departure points — Bergen city centre and Sotra/Sund — each offering unique routes with spectacular scenery. Groups up to 12 people. All safety equipment and briefing included.

Byfjorden · Bergen

Floating Sauna

Heat, cold water, fjord views

There is something deeply restorative about stepping out of a wood-fired sauna at 80–90°C and jumping straight into the cold fjord. Our floating sauna sits right on the water, with panoramic views over Bergen's rooftops and the mountains beyond.

Available all year, even in winter when the contrast between hot and cold is at its most dramatic. Book a private session, join a sauna cruise, or come for drop-in.
