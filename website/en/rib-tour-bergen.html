Guided RIB tour

An adrenaline-fuelled nature experience on the fjord

There is something extraordinary about experiencing Bergen from the sea. The Byfjord stretches from Bryggen's historic quays out towards the open Norwegian Sea, framed by green hills and dramatic mountains. A RIB tour offers a perspective that no walking tour or sightseeing bus can match — close to the water, surrounded by genuine nature.

We offer guided RIB tours from two locations: Bergen city centre at Bryggen, and from Sotra at Sund — both with unique routes that showcase the coastal landscape around Bergen at its finest. Tours are led by experienced, certified guides with deep local knowledge of the fjord, its history and the wildlife you may encounter along the way.

Whether you travel as a couple, a family or a corporate group — our RIB tours accommodate groups of up to 12 people, making the price per person highly competitive. All tours include a safety briefing, full equipment and a professional guide. No prior experience on the water is required.

Why choose us

Expert hands, spectacular routes

Local expertise

Our guides are Bergeners who know the fjord like their own backyard — from hidden coves and historic lighthouses to the best viewpoints you'll never find on a map.

Certified and safe

All guides hold valid maritime and lifesaving certificates. Life jackets and safety equipment are always included, and we keep to safe routes based on daily assessment of weather and sea conditions.

Perfect for groups

With room for up to 12 people, the RIB tour suits friends, corporate events and families alike. The more of you, the better the price per person — as low as NOK 825 with a full boat.

Flexible departure

Two departure points — Bergen city centre and Sotra/Sund — make it easy to choose what suits your group best. We adapt the meeting time to fit your schedule.

Unforgettable experience

Guests from all over the world describe the RIB tour as the highlight of their Bergen visit. The combination of speed, fresh sea air and magnificent scenery creates moments you'll remember long after you've returned home.

Everything included

Life jacket, waterproof jacket, safety briefing and a warm drink on board are all included in the price. Just bring a good mood and clothes you don't mind getting a little wet.
