Our story

From love of the sea to a way of life

It all started with an old wooden boat and a dream. Magnus Lindström grew up near Bryggen and spent his childhood rowing between the islands, crabbing with his grandfather and watching the boat traffic come and go from the Byfjord. For him, the sea was not just a backdrop — it was home. When he met Astrid Bakke during a seamanship course in 2014, he found not only his future partner, but also the co-founder of what would become Kystopplevelser.

We started small. One RIB boat, a little shed by the quay and a pile of unanswered questions about what people really wanted from a coastal experience. What we quickly learned was that guests — whether they came from Stavanger or Sydney — were looking for something genuine. Not just a boat trip, but a connection to the sea, to the local culture and to the people they were travelling with. We decided that was exactly what we would offer: authentic experiences, told by people who genuinely love the coast.

Today we are a small team of sea-obsessed Bergeners who care deeply about making sure you feel the same thing we do — every single day we are out on the fjord. We have grown slowly and deliberately, always keeping quality over quantity. Every year we welcome guests from all over the world, and every year the sea teaches us something new — as do the people we meet on it.

"The sea does something to people. It puts everything in perspective — and that is exactly what we want for you."

— Magnus Lindström, Managing Director

What we believe in

Our values

These three principles are the foundation of everything we do out on the water.

Safety

Safety is never a compromise for us. All our equipment is certified and regularly inspected. Every guide holds valid maritime and lifesaving certificates. You can relax — we look after you.

Local knowledge

We are Bergeners through and through. We know every cove, every reef and all the hidden gems along the coast. Our local expertise means you discover places most visitors never find on their own.

Sustainability

We love the sea and want to protect it. We use eco-friendly fuel wherever possible, maintain strict rules against waste in the fjord, and support local initiatives to preserve the coastal ecosystem for future generations.
