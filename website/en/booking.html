FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Feel free to contact us directly.

What happens if the weather is bad?
We monitor forecasts closely and will contact you 24 hours in advance if we need to cancel. Full refund or free rebooking is always offered — we never let bad weather ruin your plans.
What should I wear?
We recommend warm, comfortable layers and a windproof jacket — even in summer it can feel brisk out on the fjord. We can lend life jackets and rain gear. For the sauna, bring a swimsuit and towel (we can also provide towels).
Do I need a boat licence?
No! None of our activities require a boat licence. We provide all necessary instruction and a safety briefing before departure. No prior experience on the water is required.
Can children participate?
Yes! The sauna is suitable for all ages and is a family-friendly activity we love to arrange for families. RIB tours are suitable for children from age 8. Boat rental is open to the whole family — we tailor the experience to your group.
What is the cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before the agreed time. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a 50% charge. If we cancel due to weather or technical issues, you are always offered a full refund or free rebooking.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes! For groups of 8 or more and corporate clients we offer tailored packages and discounts. Contact us directly for a quote suited to your group — we love organising special experiences.
Is equipment included?
Yes! All necessary safety equipment is included in the price — from life jackets and rain gear for RIB tours to towels and sauna accessories. Just show up.
How far is it from Bergen city centre?
We are based at Bryggen — right in the heart of Bergen. It's a 5-minute walk from Bergen train station and 10 minutes from the airport bus terminal. The city tram stops at Byparken, just 2 minutes away on foot.

Still not sure? Call us or send an email — we're happy to help you choose the right experience.
